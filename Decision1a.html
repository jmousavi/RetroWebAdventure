The Story

Oh no! You did not notice the floating asteroids surrounding the planet. You try to manouver around them, but you end up getting hit. Your emergency lights turn on. Unfortuently you are so far into the cluster of asteroids that your only hope of survival is landing onto the unknow planet. You try your best to land but your rockets are not working, causing you to crash onto the planet, and pass out.

You wake up alone inside your ship, seeing emergency lights flashing. You decide to go check out your ship.
Suddenly you begin to wake up. As your eyes begin to adjust you do not recognize your surroundings and realize you are no longer in your ship.
